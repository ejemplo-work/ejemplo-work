Dry ice cleaning


At our renowned luxury car shop, we offer a revolutionary cleaning experience through the Dry Ice Cleaning service. Our dedication to innovation and excellence has led us to adopt this advanced technology that provides deep, thorough cleaning without damaging the paint or components of high-end vehicles.

Our philosophy is based on the importance of preserving the appearance and value of luxury cars while performing thorough cleanings. Dry ice, in the form of extremely cold pellets, is used to remove dirt, grease and contaminants from the vehicle's surface. By not using water or harsh chemicals, we ensure environmentally friendly cleaning that is safe for paint and finishes.

What really stands out in our Dry Ice Cleaning service is its ability to reach hard-to-reach areas and remove embedded contaminants without leaving residue. From the smallest gaps to delicate surfaces, this technology allows us to clean thoroughly without causing damage. Our team is trained to use the correct pressure and angle, ensuring each vehicle receives a deep, even clean.
Our reputation is built on the satisfaction of customers who have experienced a stunning transformation in the look and feel of their luxury cars after subjecting them to our Dry Ice Cleaning service. More info


From removing road debris to restoring the original shine, our results speak for themselves. We pride ourselves on achieving a level of cleanliness that exceeds expectations and highlights the natural beauty of each vehicle.


At every stage of the Dry Ice Cleaning process, we maintain transparent communication with our customers. Before carrying out any work, we explain the process in detail and discuss the benefits to the vehicle.
