The Verduritas Kitchen

At Verduritas we are committed to international vegan recipes, selected by our chef so that you can test your palate with new flavours from cuisines all over the world.

We want people to understand that being vegetarian or vegan doesn't mean eating boring, and at Verduritas we prove it to you.

At Verduritas we believe in healthy eating, with all the flavour.

The wines of Verduritas

As we all know, the raw material of wine is the grape, each wine can be made from different types of grapes. Therefore, up to this point it would be a "vegetable drink", suitable for any diet, both vegetarian and vegan.

In Verduritas we know that this theory is not entirely true, in the process of wine clarification many wineries often use animal products such as: gelatin, casein, albumin...

In our cellar you will only find a selection of the best vegan and organic wines with all the certifications to prove it.

The design of the Verduritas

At Verduritas you will live a unique experience of flavours and senses that you will not forget.
